# Aaryavart.org

Aaryavart Centre for Autism and Special Needs is a non-profit organization providing therapy, education, and family support for autistic and other neurodiverse children in Lucknow and nearby regions in Uttar Pradesh, India.  

Live site: https://aaryavart.org

![Aaryavart screenshot](https://aaryavart.org/assets/second.webp)

## Mission

Aaryavart focuses on helping children with autism, ADHD, and related developmental conditions build practical life skills, communication, and social abilities so they can move toward more independent and fulfilling lives.
Families receive guided support through structured programs, assessments, and therapies tailored to each child’s strengths and needs.[web:5][web:8]
